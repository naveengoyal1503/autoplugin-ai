# AffiliateLink Booster

**AffiliateLink Booster** is a WordPress plugin designed to help affiliate marketers automatically cloak and optimize their affiliate links with enhanced tracking and personalized recommendations, boosting affiliate revenue.

## Features

- Automatically detects affiliate links from specified domains in your posts and pages.
- Cloaks affiliate URLs into clean, branded redirect links on your site.
- Tracks clicks on each cloaked affiliate link with admin dashboard stats.
- Simple settings panel to specify your affiliate domains.
- Lightweight and easy to use.

## Installation

1. Upload the `affiliate-link-booster.php` file to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress.
3. Navigate to **Settings > AffiliateLink Booster** to configure your affiliate domains.

## Setup

- Enter your affiliate domains separated by commas (e.g., `amazon.com,clickbank.net,shareasale.com`).
- The plugin will automatically scan your posts and pages for URLs matching these domains and cloak them.
- Monitor clicks on your affiliate links in the plugin settings page.

## Usage

- Add affiliate URLs directly in your post content as normal.
- The plugin will replace all recognized affiliate URLs with cloaked tracking links automatically.
- Paste cloaked links anywhere you want in your site or communications for better tracking.

## Monetization Model

The plugin uses a freemium approach:
- Free: Basic affiliate link cloaking and click tracking.
- Premium (coming later): Advanced analytics, A/B testing of affiliate offers, scheduling, and personalized affiliate link recommendations to maximize revenue (via subscription).
