# ContentRank Pro

AI-powered content performance analyzer for WordPress that helps you optimize your blog posts for SEO, readability, and engagement.

## Features

- **Content Analysis**: Automatically analyze word count, SEO score, readability, and engagement metrics
- **SEO Scoring**: Get instant feedback on SEO optimization with scoring from 0-100
- **Readability Analysis**: Measure how easy your content is to read
- **Engagement Metrics**: Track engagement potential based on formatting and structure
- **Dashboard**: View analytics for all analyzed posts in one place
- **Post Column**: See ContentRank scores directly in your posts list
- **Pro Features**: AI-powered suggestions for improving your content (Pro tier)
- **Database Tracking**: All analytics are stored securely in your WordPress database

## Installation

1. Download the plugin files
2. Upload to `/wp-content/plugins/contentrank-pro/` directory
3. Activate the plugin through the WordPress Plugins menu
4. Navigate to ContentRank Pro in your admin dashboard

## Setup

No configuration needed! The plugin works out of the box:

1. Edit any post or create a new one
2. Open the ContentRank Pro meta box in the sidebar
3. Click "Analyze Content" to get instant metrics
4. View your scores and suggestions

## Usage

### Analyzing Content

- Go to Posts > Edit
- Look for the "ContentRank Pro Analysis" box on the right sidebar
- Click "Analyze Content" to generate metrics
- Review your scores:
  - **Word Count**: Total words in your post
  - **SEO Score**: How optimized your content is for search engines (0-100)
  - **Readability**: How easy your content is to read (0-100)
  - **Engagement Score**: Potential reader engagement based on formatting (0-100)

### Viewing Analytics

- Navigate to ContentRank Pro > Dashboard
- See overview statistics for all analyzed posts
- Check individual post scores in the Posts list (new "ContentRank Score" column)

### Getting Suggestions (Pro)

- Click "View AI Suggestions" in the meta box
- Receive personalized recommendations to improve your content
- Implement suggestions to boost your scores
