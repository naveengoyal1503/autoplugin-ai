# WP Revenue Tracker

Track and visualize your WordPress site's revenue from ads, affiliate links, digital sales, and memberships.

## Features
- Dashboard to log revenue by type (ads, affiliate, sales, memberships)
- Visual chart of revenue over time
- Easy to use interface

## Installation
1. Upload the plugin folder to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to the 'Revenue Tracker' menu in your admin panel.

## Setup
- Start logging revenue by selecting the type, entering the amount, and choosing the date.
- View your revenue chart on the dashboard.

## Usage
- Use the form to add new revenue entries.
- The chart updates automatically to show your revenue trends.

## Requirements
- WordPress 5.0 or higher
- JavaScript enabled in your browser
