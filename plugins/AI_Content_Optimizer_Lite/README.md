# AI Content Optimizer Lite

[![WordPress plugin](https://img.shields.io/wordpress/plugin/downloads/ai-content-optimizer-lite?style=flat-square)](https://wordpress.org/plugins/ai-content-optimizer-lite/)

**AI Content Optimizer Lite** is a powerful **freemium** WordPress plugin that uses simulated AI to analyze your post content for **readability**, **SEO**, and **engagement**. Get instant scores and suggestions to improve your content.

## Features

### Free Version
- **Content Analysis**: Paste content and get a readability/SEO score (0-100).
- **Basic Suggestions**: Tips on word count, headings, links.
- **Usage Limit**: 5 analyses per month.
- **History**: Track your recent analyses.

### Premium Features (Upgrade for $49/year)
- Unlimited analyses.
- **Advanced AI Rewrites**: Auto-optimize sentences.
- **Keyword Research**: AI-suggested keywords.
- **Bulk Analysis**: Optimize entire posts from editor.
- Priority support & regular updates.

## Installation

1. Upload the `ai-content-optimizer-lite` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to **Settings > AI Content Optimizer** to start analyzing.

## Setup

No setup required! After activation:
1. Navigate to **Settings > AI Content Optimizer**.
2. Paste your post content into the textarea.
3. Click **Analyze Content**.

## Usage

1. **Analyze New Content**: Paste draft into the tool for instant feedback.
2. **Check History**: View past scores and tips.
3. **Upgrade Prompt**: When limit reached, upgrade link appears.

**Pro Tip**: Aim for 80+ scores for best results!
