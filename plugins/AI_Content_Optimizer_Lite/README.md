# AI Content Optimizer Lite

A powerful **freemium** WordPress plugin that instantly analyzes your post content for **SEO**, **readability**, and engagement. Get a score, tips, and keyword insights in seconds. Upgrade to Premium for AI-powered rewrites and unlimited usage.

## Features
- **Content Analysis**: Instant score (0-100) based on word count, readability, and keywords[1].
- **Keyword Extraction**: Top 5 keywords from your content.
- **Readability Score**: Average sentence length for better user experience.
- **Daily Free Scans**: **5 free scans per day**; upsell for unlimited.
- **Post Integration**: Works seamlessly from Tools > AI Content Optimizer or edit screens.
- **Premium Upsell**: Built-in notices linking to your premium sales page.

**Premium Features** (sold separately): Unlimited scans, AI content suggestions, meta tag optimizer, bulk processing.

## Installation
1. Download the plugin ZIP.
2. In WordPress admin: Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin.
3. Activate **AI Content Optimizer Lite**.
4. Access via Tools > AI Content Optimizer.

## Setup
- No configuration needed for free version.
- Paste post content and click **Analyze**.
- View results and apply tips manually.

## Usage
1. Go to **Tools > AI Content Optimizer**.
2. Paste or edit your post content.
3. Click **Analyze Content**.
4. Review **score**, stats, and tips.
5. **Free limit**: 5 scans/day. Upgrade for more!

**Pro Tip**: Use on draft posts for instant improvements. Target score: 80+ for optimal SEO.
