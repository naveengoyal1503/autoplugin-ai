# ContentMoner Pro - WordPress Monetization Hub

## Overview

ContentMoner Pro is an all-in-one WordPress monetization solution that empowers content creators, bloggers, and site owners to diversify and maximize their revenue streams. This unified platform combines affiliate link management, sponsored content tracking, ad optimization, and digital product sales in a single, intuitive dashboard.

## Features

### Core Features
- **Affiliate Link Manager**: Create, manage, and track unlimited affiliate programs with custom links and commission rates
- **Click & Conversion Tracking**: Real-time analytics dashboard showing clicks, conversions, and revenue per affiliate program
- **Digital Product Sales**: Sell eBooks, courses, and digital downloads directly from your WordPress site
- **Sponsored Content Module**: Track and manage sponsored posts and branded content collaborations
- **Revenue Dashboard**: Comprehensive overview of total clicks, conversions, and earnings across all monetization channels
- **Ad Network Integration Ready**: Built-in support for Google AdSense, Monetag, and other ad networks
- **Shortcode System**: Simple shortcodes for embedding affiliate links and product listings into posts and pages

### Advanced Features (Premium)
- Unlimited affiliate program management
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Email notifications for new conversions
- A/B testing for affiliate links
- Automated payout reports
- Priority support

## Installation

1. Log in to your WordPress admin panel
2. Navigate to **Plugins > Add New**
3. Search for "ContentMoner Pro"
4. Click **Install Now** and then **Activate**
5. You'll see a new menu item "ContentMoner Pro" in the left sidebar

### Manual Installation

1. Download the ContentMoner Pro plugin zip file
2. Go to **Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin**
3. Upload the zip file and click **Install Now**
4. Activate the plugin

## Setup Guide

### Initial Configuration

1. **Access the Dashboard**: Click on "ContentMoner Pro" in the left sidebar
2. **View Statistics**: The main dashboard displays your total clicks, revenue, active programs, and digital products
3. **Configure Settings**: Go to **Settings** tab to set your license type and preferences

### Adding Affiliate Programs

1. Navigate to **Affiliate Links** from the main menu
2. Fill in the following details:
   - **Program Name**: e.g., "Amazon Associates", "Shopify Affiliate"
   - **Affiliate URL**: Your unique affiliate link from the program
   - **Commission Rate**: The percentage commission you earn (e.g., 5, 10, 20)
3. Click **Add Affiliate Program**
4. Your program appears in the list below with a custom tracking link

### Creating Digital Products

1. Go to **Digital Products** section
2. Enter product details:
   - **Product Name**: Title of your product
   - **Price**: Set your product price
   - **Description**: Detailed product description
3. Click **Add Product**
4. Use the generated shortcode to embed the product in your posts

### Managing Sponsored Content

1. Go to **ContentMoner Pro > Sponsored Content**
2. Create a new sponsored post with brand details, compensation, and content requirements
3. Track performance and manage multiple sponsorship agreements

## Usage

### Embedding Affiliate Links

Use this shortcode in your posts and pages:


[contentmoner_affiliate_link id="1" text="Check out this amazing product"]


Replace `id` with your affiliate program ID and customize the link text as needed.

### Embedding Digital Products

Display your products with:


[contentmoner_digital_product id="1"]


This renders a product card with title, description, price, and purchase button.

### Viewing Analytics

1. Click **Analytics** in the main menu
2. View performance metrics:
   - Total clicks per affiliate program
   - Conversion rates
   - Revenue generated
   - Date-range filtering for custom reports

### Creating Sponsored Content Posts

1. Go to **ContentMoner Pro > Sponsored Content**
2. Click "Create New Sponsored Post"
3. Add post title, content, and brand information
4. Set compensation amount and track performance

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I track affiliate clicks?**  
A: The plugin automatically tracks clicks when users use your affiliate link shortcodes. Visit the Analytics tab to view detailed click data.

**Q: Can I use this with WooCommerce?**  
A: Yes! ContentMoner Pro works alongside WooCommerce to complement your e-commerce monetization strategy.

**Q: Is there a limit to affiliate programs I can add?**  
A: The free version allows 3 programs. Premium and Pro versions support unlimited programs.

**Q: How do I export my analytics?**  
A: Use the Analytics page to generate custom reports that can be exported as CSV.

**Q: Does this work with all WordPress themes?**  
A: Yes, ContentMoner Pro is compatible with all WordPress themes and works seamlessly in the background.
