# WP Revenue Booster

Maximize your WordPress site's revenue with smart affiliate, ad, and sponsored content rotation.

## Features
- Create and manage revenue campaigns for affiliate links, ads, and sponsored content.
- Rotate content variants to optimize for conversions.
- Track clicks and performance per campaign and variant.
- Shortcode integration for easy placement in posts and pages.

## Installation
1. Upload the `wp-revenue-booster.php` file to your WordPress plugins directory (`/wp-content/plugins/`).
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to the 'Revenue Booster' menu to create your first campaign.

## Setup
1. Create a new Revenue Campaign from the admin menu.
2. Add your affiliate links, ad codes, or sponsored content in the editor. Separate multiple variants with `||`.
3. Use the shortcode `[revenue_booster campaign="Campaign Name" type="affiliate|ad|sponsored"]` in your posts or pages.

## Usage
- Place the shortcode where you want the rotating content to appear.
- Monitor campaign performance in the admin dashboard.
- Adjust your campaigns based on click data to maximize revenue.
