# WP Revenue Booster

Automatically optimize ad placement, affiliate links, and upsell offers to maximize your site's revenue.

## Features
- Smart ad placement optimization
- Affiliate link management
- Upsell offer integration
- Easy-to-use admin settings

## Installation
1. Upload the plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Settings > Revenue Booster to configure your preferences.

## Setup
- Enable or disable ad optimization, affiliate links, and upsell offers in the plugin settings.
- Customize the appearance and placement as needed.

## Usage
- The plugin will automatically inject optimized content into your site based on your settings.
- Monitor your revenue growth and adjust settings as needed.
