# ContentBoost AI Writer

## Description

ContentBoost AI Writer is a powerful WordPress plugin that leverages artificial intelligence to generate high-quality, SEO-optimized content directly from your WordPress dashboard. Perfect for bloggers, ecommerce store owners, and content marketers who want to scale their content production without sacrificing quality.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Content Generation**: Generate blog posts, product descriptions, email copy, and social media content with a single click
- **Multiple Content Types**: Support for blog posts, product descriptions, email marketing copy, and social media posts
- **Tone Customization**: Choose from professional, casual, friendly, or persuasive tones to match your brand voice
- **Usage Tracking**: Daily generation limits with real-time usage monitoring
- **Freemium Model**: Start free with 5 generations per day, upgrade to premium for unlimited access
- **Easy Integration**: Seamlessly inserts generated content into your WordPress posts
- **Dashboard Analytics**: Track your daily usage and plan status

## Installation

1. Download the ContentBoost AI Writer plugin
2. Navigate to **Plugins > Add New** in your WordPress dashboard
3. Click **Upload Plugin** and select the plugin file
4. Click **Install Now** and then **Activate**
5. The plugin will automatically create necessary database tables

## Setup

### Initial Configuration

1. Go to **ContentBoost AI > Settings** in the WordPress admin menu
2. Configure your API keys if connecting to external AI services (OpenAI, Claude, etc.)
3. Set your preferred default tone and content style
4. Save your settings

### User Plans

- **Free**: 5 generations per day
- **Premium**: $9.99/month for 50 generations per day
- **Enterprise**: Custom limits available

## Usage

### Generating Content

1. Click **ContentBoost AI** in the WordPress admin menu
2. Enter your topic or keyword in the "Topic or Keyword" field
3. Select your desired **Content Type** from the dropdown:
   - Blog Post: Long-form, comprehensive articles
   - Product Description: Conversion-focused product details
   - Email Copy: Engaging email marketing content
   - Social Media Post: Platform-optimized social content
4. Choose your preferred **Tone**:
   - Professional: For business and technical content
   - Casual: Relaxed and conversational style
   - Friendly: Warm and approachable tone
   - Persuasive: Sales and conversion-focused
5. Click **Generate Content**
6. Review the generated content in the result box
7. Use **Copy to Clipboard** to copy the content, or **Insert into Post** to add it directly to your current post

### Monitoring Usage

Your daily usage is displayed in the dashboard header with a visual progress bar. When you reach your daily limit, you'll need to upgrade your plan to continue generating content.
