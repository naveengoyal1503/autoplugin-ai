# Smart AI Content Optimizer

## Features

- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Instantly scores your content for **SEO**, readability, and engagement.
- **One-Click Optimization**: Get rewritten content with improved structure, keywords, and calls-to-action.
- **Free Tier**: Analyze up to **5 posts per month** at no cost.
- **Premium Features**:
  - Unlimited optimizations.
  - Advanced keyword suggestions.
  - Full AI rewrites and A/B test previews.
  - Export reports for teams.
- **Seamless Integration**: Works directly in the WordPress post editor.
- **Privacy-Focused**: All processing done locally (premium uses secure API).

## Installation

1. Download the plugin ZIP from [your site](https://example.com).
2. In your WordPress admin, go to **Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin**.
3. Upload and activate **Smart AI Content Optimizer**.
4. Edit any post and find the **AI Content Optimizer** sidebar.

## Setup

1. **Free Use**: Click **Optimize Content with AI** up to 5 times/month.
2. **Go Premium**:
   - Visit **Settings > AI Content Optimizer**.
   - Enter your premium key (get at [premium.example.com](https://example.com/premium)).
   - Or subscribe directly: **$9.99/month** or **$99/year** (20% savings).

## Usage

1. Edit a **Post** or **Page**.
2. In the sidebar, click **Optimize Content with AI**.
3. Review **scores** (SEO, Readability) and **suggestions**.
4. Copy the **optimized content** into your editor.
5. Publish and watch traffic grow!

**Pro Tip**: Use on draft posts for best results. Premium users get real-time keyword integration.
