# WP Smart Upsell Engine

Boost your store's revenue with intelligent upsell and cross-sell recommendations powered by visitor behavior and purchase history.

## Features
- Automatically recommends relevant products after purchase or on product pages
- Easy-to-use admin settings
- Works with WooCommerce
- Increases average order value

## Installation
1. Upload the plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Ensure WooCommerce is installed and activated

## Setup
1. Go to Settings > Smart Upsell in your WordPress admin
2. Configure upsell rules and preferences
3. Assign upsell products to your items in WooCommerce

## Usage
- The plugin will automatically display upsell recommendations on product pages and after purchase
- Customize recommendations and settings from the admin panel

## Requirements
- WordPress 5.6+
- WooCommerce 5.0+
