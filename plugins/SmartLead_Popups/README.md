# SmartLead Popups

AI-powered popup builder that creates personalized lead capture forms dynamically optimizing visitor engagement and conversions.

## Features

- Easy-to-use popup lead capture form
- AI-powered copy suggestions and personalization (premium)
- Multi-step forms support (premium)
- A/B testing for popup variants (premium)
- Integration with major email marketing platforms (MailChimp, ConvertKit, etc.)
- Responsive and lightweight design
- GDPR compliant email capture

## Installation

1. Upload the `smartlead-popups.php` file to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. The popup will automatically appear on your website.

## Setup

- The free version displays a simple popup for email capture.
- Configure integration settings and AI personalization features in the premium settings panel (coming soon).

## Usage

- Visitors see a popup after 5 seconds with an invitation to subscribe.
- They enter their email address which is validated and (optionally) sent to your email marketing service.
- Upon successful subscription, a thank you message is displayed.
- Use premium add-ons to customize popups, create funnels, and integrate with other marketing tools.

## FAQ

**Q:** Can I customize the popup look?

**A:** Currently basic styling is included; premium version will support advanced customization.

**Q:** Does it support GDPR compliance?

**A:** Yes, the email field requires explicit input; you can add additional consent fields in premium version.

**Q:** Which email marketing platforms are supported?

**A:** Free version allows basic capture; premium supports MailChimp, ConvertKit, AWeber, and others.
