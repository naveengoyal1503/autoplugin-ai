# AI Content Optimizer Pro

## Description
**AI Content Optimizer Pro** is a powerful WordPress plugin that uses AI to analyze and optimize your blog posts and pages for better SEO performance. The free version provides basic content scoring and suggestions, while premium unlocks advanced AI rewriting, bulk processing, and detailed analytics.

Boost your content rankings effortlessly!

## Features

### Free Version
- **Content Analysis**: Get an instant SEO score (0-100%) based on word count, keyword presence, and structure.
- **Actionable Suggestions**: Improve readability, length, and keyword usage with simple tips.
- **Post/Page Integration**: Side meta box in WordPress editor for quick analysis.

### Premium Features ($9/mo or $99/yr)
- **AI Auto-Rewriting**: Automatically rewrite content for optimal SEO.
- **Bulk Optimization**: Process multiple posts at once.
- **Competitor Analysis**: Compare your content against top-ranking pages.
- **Advanced Analytics Dashboard**: Track improvements over time.
- **Priority Support & Regular Updates**.

## Installation
1. Upload the `ai-content-optimizer` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Edit any post/page – you'll see the **AI Content Optimizer** meta box on the side.

## Setup
1. Go to **Settings > AI Content Optimizer** (or **Tools > AI Content Optimizer** in some setups).
2. **Free**: Ready to use!
3. **Premium**: Enter your license key from [your purchase](https://example.com/pricing).

## Usage
1. Open a post or page in the editor.
2. Click **Analyze Content** in the AI Content Optimizer box.
3. View your **SEO Score** and follow **Suggestions** to improve.
4. **Premium Users**: Enable AI rewrite for instant optimizations.
