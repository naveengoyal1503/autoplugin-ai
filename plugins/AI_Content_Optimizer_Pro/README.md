# AI Content Optimizer Pro

A powerful **freemium** WordPress plugin that leverages AI to analyze and optimize your content for **readability**, **SEO**, and **engagement**.

## Features

### Free Version
- **Readability Score**: Instant Flesch Reading Ease analysis on posts/pages.
- **Meta Box Integration**: Analyze directly from the post editor.
- **Unlimited Basic Scans**: No limits on core analysis.

### Premium Features ($9/mo or $99/yr)
- **AI-Powered Rewrites**: Automatically improve content clarity and engagement.
- **Bulk Optimization**: Optimize multiple posts at once.
- **Advanced SEO Insights**: Keyword suggestions and density analysis.
- **Priority Support**: Fast email support.
- **Unlimited Usage**: No post limits.

## Installation

1. Upload the `ai-content-optimizer` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress admin.
3. Edit any post/page â€“ find the **AI Content Optimizer** meta box in the sidebar.

## Setup

1. Go to **Settings > AI Content Optimizer** (or **Tools > AI Content Optimizer**).
2. **Free**: Click 'Analyze Content' in the post editor.
3. **Premium**: Enter your license key to activate advanced features.

## Usage

1. **Basic Analysis**:
   - Open a post/page editor.
   - Click **Analyze Content (Free)** in the sidebar meta box.
   - View your **Readability Score** (0-100%).

2. **Premium Optimization**:
   - Activate premium with your key.
   - Re-analyze to get AI rewrite suggestions.
