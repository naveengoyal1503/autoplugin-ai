# AI Content Optimizer

A powerful **AI-powered plugin** to analyze and optimize your WordPress posts for **readability**, **SEO**, and **engagement**. Free version offers basic scoring; premium unlocks AI rewriting and more.

## Features

### Free Version
- **Readability Score**: Instant analysis of content complexity.
- **SEO & Engagement Suggestions**: Actionable tips to improve posts.
- **Usage Limit**: 5 optimizations per month.

### Premium Features ($9/month)
- **Unlimited Optimizations**: No limits.
- **AI Content Rewriting**: Automatically improve text.
- **Bulk Processing**: Optimize multiple posts at once.
- **Advanced SEO Metrics**: Keyword density, meta suggestions.
- **Priority Support**: Fast email help.

## Installation

1. Upload the `ai-content-optimizer` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`.
2. Activate the plugin through the **Plugins** menu in WordPress.
3. Edit any post/page; the optimizer box appears in the sidebar.

## Setup

1. Go to **Settings > AI Optimizer**.
2. Enter your **premium license key** (if purchased) to unlock pro features.
3. Usage tracked monthly; resets automatically.

## Usage

1. Edit a **post** or **page**.
2. In the **AI Content Optimizer** sidebar box, click **Analyze & Optimize**.
3. View your **score** and **suggestions**.
4. Premium users: Enable rewrite in settings for auto-improvements.
