# AffiliateSmartLink

Automatically converts product links in your posts into affiliate links enhanced with coupons and cashback tooltips to boost your affiliate marketing revenue.

## Features

- Automatically detect Amazon product links and add your affiliate tag.
- Display tooltip near links showing "Coupon & Cashback Available" to increase clicks and conversions.
- Lightweight and self-contained plugin.
- Supports WordPress posts and pages content.

## Installation

1. Upload the `AffiliateSmartLink.php` file to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Edit the plugin PHP file to insert your Amazon affiliate ID in the `$affiliate_id` variable.

## Setup

No additional setup required. The plugin works automatically on your existing and new posts.

## Usage

Just create posts with Amazon product links as usual. AffiliateSmartLink will automatically append your affiliate ID and display a subtle tooltip promoting coupons and cashback options.

This boosts user confidence and conversion rates without changing your workflow.
