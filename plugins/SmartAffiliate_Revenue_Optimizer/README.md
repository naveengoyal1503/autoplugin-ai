# SmartAffiliate Revenue Optimizer

## Description

SmartAffiliate Revenue Optimizer is a powerful WordPress plugin designed to help bloggers and content creators maximize their affiliate marketing earnings. It provides intelligent affiliate link management, performance tracking, and optimization tools to increase conversion rates and revenue.

## Features

- **Affiliate Link Management**: Easily add, organize, and manage all your affiliate links from one centralized dashboard
- **Performance Tracking**: Monitor clicks, conversions, and revenue for each affiliate link in real-time
- **Analytics Dashboard**: View top-performing affiliate products and conversion rates
- **Click Tracking**: Automatically track visitor clicks on your affiliate links
- **Shortcode Support**: Use `[smart_affiliate id="affiliate_id"]` to insert links anywhere in your content
- **Dashboard Widget**: Quick stats widget for immediate revenue visibility
- **Conversion Rate Optimization**: Track CTR (click-through rates) to identify high-performing content
- **Revenue Reports**: Monitor total affiliate earnings and performance trends

## Installation

1. Upload the plugin folder to `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress
3. Navigate to SmartAffiliate in the main menu to access the plugin dashboard

## Setup

1. Go to **SmartAffiliate > Settings**
2. Enter your affiliate API key (optional, for advanced features)
3. Enable click tracking to monitor link performance
4. Toggle auto-insert to automatically add affiliate links to your content
5. Save your settings

## Usage

### Adding Affiliate Links

1. Go to **SmartAffiliate > Affiliate Links**
2. Fill in the link details:
   - **Affiliate ID**: Unique identifier for the link (e.g., "amazon_laptop")
   - **Product Name**: Name of the product being promoted
   - **Affiliate URL**: Your affiliate tracking URL
   - **Commission Rate**: Your expected commission (e.g., "5%")
3. Click "Add Link" to save

### Using Shortcodes

Add affiliate links to your posts and pages using:


[smart_affiliate id="affiliate_id"]


Replace `affiliate_id` with your unique affiliate ID.

### Monitoring Performance

1. Visit **SmartAffiliate > Analytics** to see your top-performing links
2. Check the dashboard for quick revenue stats
3. Review conversion rates to optimize your content strategy
